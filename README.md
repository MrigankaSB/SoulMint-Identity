# SoulMint Identity

## Project Description
SoulMint Identity is a modular on-chain identity management protocol built on the Ethereum blockchain. Designed for the decentralized future, it allows users to mint immutable, non-transferable “soulbound” identity NFTs. Enhanced with extensibility for custom metadata (attributes), verifiable credentials, and off-chain proof verification, SoulMint Identity establishes a foundation for reputation, authentication, and credentialing systems in a trustless web3 ecosystem.

## Project Vision
To provide every individual with a decentralized, verifiable, and censorship-resistant digital identity that is recognized and trusted across dApps, DAOs, and decentralized societies. SoulMint Identity will serve as the universal passport for digital sovereignty, enabling secure identity, credential management, and proof-of-existence across chains and platforms.

## Key Features

- **Soulbound Identity NFT:** Each address can mint one immutable, non-transferable identity NFT with customizable name and metadata URI.
- **Modular Attributes:** Users can attach an unlimited array of key-value attributes to enrich their identity profiles.
- **Verifiable Credentials (VCs):** Admins or delegated issuers can confer credentials (badges, attestations) to identities, each verifiably tied to the identity NFT.
- **Proof Submission (Signature Verification):** Supports off-chain signature proofs for statements or actions, verifiable on-chain for flexible authentication and integrations.
- **Public Access and Querying:** Retrieve identities, attributes, credentials by address or identity ID.
- **Governance & Delegation:** Admin controls for transferring contract ownership and authorizing credential issuers.
- **Event-rich Design:** Extensive events for all core actions, enabling comprehensive off-chain indexing, UI/UX integration, and analytics.
- **Extensible Architecture:** Clean structuring for future support of privacy-preserving proofs, governance, and advanced social or DAO reputation protocols.

## Future Scope

- **Privacy Preserving Extensions:** Integration with zero-knowledge proof schemes for selective disclosure and confidential identity claims.
- **Interchain & Cross-Platform Identity:** Adapters to extend SoulMint identities across multiple EVM and non-EVM networks.
- **Social and DAO Reputation:** Native support for social graph, trust/reputation scoring, and governance delegation.
- **Credential Recovery & Endorsement:** Account recovery flows, multi-party endorsements, and trusted third-party verification.
- **Decentralized Indexing and Discovery:** Advanced on-chain and off-chain search, filtering, and analytics tools.
- **Wider Standards Support:** Compliance with ERC-725/735, DIDs, and verifiable credential ecosystem standards for maximal interoperability.
